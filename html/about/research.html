The Atlas of Economic Complexity: Mapping Paths to Prosperity

The Growth Lab applied its theories of Economic Complexity to compile an extensive dataset ranking 128 countries in terms of economic complexity and their likelihood for growth by 2020. The Growth Lab published these rankings and country product maps in The Atlas of Economic Complexity: Mapping Paths to Prosperity, a breakthrough study that offers innovative maps of national growth trajectories and analyses for countries to consult when considering their own paths to national prosperity. This study was led by Ricardo Hausmann, director of CID, and Cesar A. Hidalgo, Asahi Broadcast Corporation Career Development Professor at MIT.
